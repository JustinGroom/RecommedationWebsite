2026 SEO playbook for affiliate recommendation sites

Updated April 28, 2026. Based on recent Google Search Central and FTC guidance.

  1. People-first beats volume-first: only publish pages that solve a real buying decision.
  2. Avoid scaled-content abuse risk: use automation for drafting and enrichment, then add unique testing insights before publishing.
  3. Use structured data: implement Article + Product-related fields where relevant for richer eligibility in results.
  4. Build topic clusters: connect homepage → category pages → article pages with contextual internal links.
  5. Protect monetization accounts: avoid deceptive ad placements and prevent accidental ad clicks.
  6. Disclose affiliate relationships clearly: place disclosure near recommendation content and affiliate links.
