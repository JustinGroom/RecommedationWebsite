2026 SEO + Monetization Playbook

Built from recent Google Search Central and FTC guidance.

  1. People-first editorial: Every post should solve a real buying need, not just target a keyword.
  2. Structured data everywhere: Add Article and Product markup to improve eligibility for rich results.
  3. Programmatic + human review: Use trend automation scripts, then add editor notes and testing criteria.
  4. E-E-A-T signals: Author pages, update dates, and clear test methodology.
  5. Conversion discipline: Above-the-fold comparison tables, one clear CTA, and fast mobile loading.
  6. Compliance: Affiliate disclosure near links, and no deceptive ad placements.
